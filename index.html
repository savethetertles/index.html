What Can You Do to Protect Sea Turtle Habitat?

Don't Litter!

Reduce marine debris that may entangle or be accidentally eaten by sea turtles

Help Clean Up!

Participate in coastal clean-ups and reduce plastic use to keep our beaches and ocean clean

Reuse!

Carry reusable water bottles and shopping bags.

Be Responsible!

Do not disturb nesting turtles, nests, or hatchlings. Attend organized sea turtle watches that know how to safely observe nesting sea turtles.

Fill in holes and knock down sandcastles before you leave the beach. They can become obstacles for nesting turtles or emerging hatchlings.

You can do so much!

Helping those turtles help a lot, they are such cute adorable small creatures of elegance. Please don't harm them too much, they cause no harm to us and they take so much trauma in the beginning of their lives to survive.
